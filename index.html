BITS

The first ever task-based intra-school ICT competition organized by ACICTS


What is BITS?

BITS 2018, the Intra-School practice-based ICT competition is organized by the Ananda College ICT Society for the first time in Sri Lanka with the intention of providing platform to our school students and polish their IT knowledge in the different fields of ICT. Students have to register for the competition and claim tasks. Then they have to select a task, complete it and then submit. There are 3 tasks categories , Code, Design, Explore. All the tasks were divided into according to the above categoeries. Competitors were able to take any task from any categoery. In addition to the tasks, there were Events which the competitors could gain more points by completing. They were as follows.

The online test
ARDROBERRY
Programmer & Game Developer
Still & Motion Graphics

Our mission is to increase the number of participants in Google Code-in (GCI) competition; the world famous competition for young students which is annualy organized by Google. So we are organizing a GCI workshop during the Awarding Ceremony of the BITS. We have planned to invite other leading schools in Sri Lanka to take part in this great occasion. This workshop will be conducted by the Google Developer Group and other leading organizations and individuals in the ICT sector.

 
     

Events

The Online Test

A online test is appearing on every friday. Competitors have to answer all the questions in the given test


ADROBERRY

Competitor has  to make any embedded system for a question in the 
social using Arduino, Microbit or Raspberry.

Programmer & Game Developer

Competitor has to design any software using any language(Ex: VB, VB.net, C#, C+, C++, HTML, Java, JS, PHP, Pascal etc.) for 'Software developing' category.  

Still  & 
Motion Graphics

Competitor has to design any design related to above categories using any software

Eg : Photoshop, 3Ds Max, Blender, AfterEffects etc.
